Paneer MO:MO:

Momo are bite-size dumplings made with a spoonful of stuffing wrapped in dough. Momo are usually steamed, though they are sometimes fried or steam-fried. Meat or vegetables fillings becomes succulent as it produces an intensively flavored broth sealed inside the wrappers.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Dry Onion
  • Ginger, garlic(paste)
  • Coriander(chooped)
  • Cumin Powder
  • salt
  • Garam masala
  • Momo masala

Recipes

  1. Firstly, grate the paneer
  2. Chop and boil the cabbage and carrot
  3. Chop dry and spring onions and keep them seperately
  4. Mix the paneer, cabbage, carrot, onions and coriander
  5. Prepare dought of refined wheat flour and make small lumps
  6. Put mixture in it and make dumpling in the shapes as you like
  7. It can be steamed or fried and once it becomes ready serve with tomato pickle.
